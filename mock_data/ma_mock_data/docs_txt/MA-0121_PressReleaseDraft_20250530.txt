Northstar Industrial Analytics Inc. to Acquire Riverbend Sensorworks Ltd.

Northstar Industrial Analytics Inc. today announced it has entered into a definitive
agreement to acquire Riverbend Sensorworks Ltd., a provider of wireless sensor hardware +
embedded firmware. The combination will deliver an integrated sensor-to-analytics platform
for industrial customers.

Transaction details:
- Terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
- The transaction is expected to close in Q2 2025, subject to customary conditions.

Quotes:
“Our customers are asking for end-to-end visibility,” said Ethan Park, HR Director.
“Riverbend accelerates our ability to deliver that.”

“Northstar will help us scale,” said Noah Bennett, Head of Firmware. “Together we can
innovate faster and serve more operators.”
