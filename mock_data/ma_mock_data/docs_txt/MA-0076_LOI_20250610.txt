LETTER OF INTENT (DRAFT, NON-BINDING EXCEPT AS NOTED)
Date: June 10, 2025
Parties: Northstar Industrial Analytics Inc. (“Buyer”) and Riverbend Sensorworks Ltd.
(“Company”)

1. Transaction. Buyer proposes to acquire 100% of the Company’s equity.
2. Consideration. Enterprise Value: $134M, subject to net working capital and debt-like
adjustments.
3. Earn-out. Up to $19M based on (a) ARR growth and (b) sensor-unit shipments over 12 months
post-close.
4. Exclusivity. Company agrees to an exclusivity period of 60 days (binding).
5. Diligence. Company to provide access to data room, management interviews, and customer
references.
6. Confidentiality. Existing NDA remains in effect (binding).
7. Expenses. Each party bears its own costs; advisors are responsible for their own fees
unless otherwise agreed.

Open points:
- Definitions of ARR and shipments (timing, returns, cancellations).
- Treatment of existing customer prepayments and deferred revenue.
- Escrow / indemnity cap and basket.

We modeled a base case with 18% ARR CAGR and a downside case with delayed component
availability impacting shipments.
