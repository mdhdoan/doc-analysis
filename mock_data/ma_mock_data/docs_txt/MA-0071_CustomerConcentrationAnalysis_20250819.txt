CUSTOMER CONCENTRATION ANALYSIS (INTERNAL)
Date: 2025-08-19
Stage: Marketing

Summary:
- Top-1 customer: 27% of revenue
- Top-5 customers: 46% of revenue
- Renewal schedule: two key accounts renew within 12 months

Key observations:
- Contracts often bundle hardware shipments with annual maintenance.
- Price escalation clauses vary; some include caps tied to CPI.
- Churn risk is highest where deployments are small and channel-driven.

Mitigations:
- Prioritize executive outreach to top accounts within 30 days post-close.
- Offer roadmap visibility and bundle incentives for multi-year renewals.
- Build a pipeline heatmap to diversify mid-market segment.
