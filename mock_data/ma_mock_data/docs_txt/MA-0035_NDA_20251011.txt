MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (DRAFT)

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of October 11, 2025
between Northstar Industrial Analytics Inc. (“Disclosing Party” or “Northstar”) and
Riverbend Sensorworks Ltd. (“Receiving Party” or “Riverbend”).

1. Purpose. The parties wish to explore a potential business transaction, including a
merger, acquisition, or other strategic relationship (“Transaction”).
2. Confidential Information. Includes non-public technical, financial, customer, employee,
and operational information disclosed in any form.
3. Exclusions. Information that is publicly available, independently developed, or
rightfully received from a third party is excluded.
4. Obligations. Receiving Party shall protect Confidential Information using at least
reasonable care and not disclose it except to Representatives who need to know.
5. Term. Confidentiality obligations survive for 3 years from the Effective Date.
6. Return/Destruction. Upon request, Receiving Party will return or destroy Confidential
Information, subject to legal retention requirements.
7. No License / No Obligation. No rights are granted except as expressly stated. Neither
party is obligated to proceed with the Transaction.
