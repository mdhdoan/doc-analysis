LEGAL MEMO — PURCHASE AGREEMENT REDLINE SUMMARY (INTERNAL)
Date: 2025-05-11
Stage: Diligence
Prepared by: Miles Grant (Pine & Alder Legal LLP)

Key redline themes:
1) Representations & warranties
- IP ownership: request stronger rep on firmware source code and contractor assignments.
- Compliance: include privacy/security reps appropriate for device telemetry and support
portals.

2) Indemnities
- Proposed cap: 10% of enterprise value; survival 18 months for general reps.
- Special indemnity for known issues: customer concentration renewal risk and open-source
license audit findings.

3) Covenants
- Employee retention plan and non-solicitation.
- Interim operations: limitations on new supplier commitments without Buyer consent.

4) Closing conditions
- Bring-down of reps; receipt of key third-party consents (top customer + major supplier).

Open questions:
- Definition of “Material Adverse Effect”
- Earn-out dispute resolution and audit rights

Note:
- Redlines should be consolidated; propose using clause-by-clause comments rather than
inline email edits.
