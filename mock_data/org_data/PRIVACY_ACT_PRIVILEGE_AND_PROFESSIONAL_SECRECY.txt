Privilege and Professional Secrecy
Protected information — solicitors, advocates and
notaries
27 The head of a government institution may refuse to
disclose any personal information requested under subsection 12(1) that is subject to solicitor-client privilege or
the professional secrecy of advocates and notaries or to
litigation privilege.
R.S., 1985, c. P-21, s. 27; 2019, c. 18, s. 49.
